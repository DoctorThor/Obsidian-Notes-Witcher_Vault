### Short Rest
Short rests are 8 hours. Players may spend up to a quarter of their hit dice to heal during a short rest. 

### Full Rest
Full rests are 24 hours in a secured location. Players may spend up to half their hit dice during a Full rest. They also regain half of any class features that would normally reset on a long rest. For spellcasters this represents a number of spell slots equal to half their slot levels rounded up.

### Long Rest
Long rests are a week of non-combat activities. During this period players may create things, conduct research, travel, ect. Players may spend any amount of hit dice during the Long rest. Players regain all spent hit dice at the end of a long rest. All class abilities reset.

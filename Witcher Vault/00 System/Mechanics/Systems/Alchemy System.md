# **WITCHER ALCHEMY SYSTEM (Monster-Component–Based)**

---

# **1. Monster Components Overview**

Every monster has **1–3 component types**:

### **▪ Common Component (CC)**

Meat, claws, hides, ichor — basic alchemical base material.  
**Value:** 5–20₡

### **▪ Alchemical Extract (AE)**

Eyes, glands, venom sacs, albedo/rubedo sources.  
**Value:** 20–80₡

### **▪ Mutagen (M)**

The potent, unstable organ tied to the monster’s essence.  
**Value:** 60–450₡ depending on rarity

Mutagens are always optional drops (harvesting check).

---

# **2. Harvesting System**

After defeating a creature, the player may harvest components.

### **Skill Check:**

**Harvesting = Wisdom + proficiency (if you have herbalism or monster lore)**  
DC depends on monster type:

|Monster Type|Harvest DC|
|---|---|
|Necrophages (ghoul, alghoul)|12|
|Humanoids (drowner, nekkers)|12|
|Beasts (wolves, bears)|10|
|Cursed Ones|14|
|Insectoids|14|
|Specters / Wraiths|15|
|Vampires|16|
|Elementa|16|
|Draconids / Hybrids|16–18|
|Relicts|16|
|Higher Vampires / Legends|20+|

### **Success =**

- 1 Common Component
    
- 1 Alchemical Extract
    
- On 15+: gain **mutagen** (if creature has one)
    

### **Failure =**

- Only 1 Common Component
    
- On failure by 5+: component spoiled
    

### **Critical Fail:**

- You take 1d4 poison/necrotic damage (depending on monster)
    

### **Critical Success:**

- Gain all 3 categories
    
- Mutagen is higher-grade (upgrade rarity)

# **4. Alchemical Categories**

All alchemical crafting uses **3 elements** from Witcher lore:  
**Vitriol, Rebis, Aether, Quebrith, Hydragenum, Vermillion**  
(You don’t need full complexity — components automatically count as one or more.)

### **Each category requires:**

- Base alcohol (Dwarven Spirit or Albedo/Rubedo/Nigredo base)
    
- Monster components
    
- Herbs (assume easily bought or gathered)
    
- Time to craft
    

---

# **5. Crafting Rules (Simple & Fast)**

### **Crafting Check:**

**Intelligence (Alchemy) or Herbalism Tools + Proficiency**  
DC = 10 + potency tier

|Item Tier|Example|DC|Time|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Common**|Swallow, Thunderbolt|12|10 minutes|
|**Enhanced**|Enhanced oils, bombs|14|30 minutes|
|**Superior**|Superior potions, advanced bombs|16|1 hour|
|**Decoction**|Any decoction|18|2 hours|
|**Relic**|Higher Vampire decoction|20–22|4+ hours|

### **On failure:**

- Half ingredients lost
    
- Potion created but weaker (+ GM discretion)
    

### **On critical fail:**

- Components destroyed
    
- 1d6 poison/fire damage (bomb mishap etc.)

# **ALCHEMY CRAFTING STATIONS (For Camps, Cities, and Witcher Keeps)**

---

## **🧪 FIELD KIT (Portable)**

- Weight: ~5 lbs
    
- Provides: grinding, heating, combining
    
- Allows crafting **up to Enhanced** items
    
- **Disadvantage** on Superior or Decoctions
    
- Cost: **40₡**
    

Perfect for travel.

---

## **⚗️ HERBALIST LAB (Shop / Cabin)**

- Clean workspace, mortar sets, distillation glass
    
- Craft **all potions and basic decoctions** normally
    
- Bombs allowed
    
- Cost to rent: **10₡/hr**
    
- Craft advantage against poisons/venoms
    

---

## **🜂 ALCHEMICAL STILL (Advanced)**

- Alcohol purification
    
- Mutagen stabilizer
    
- Explosion-proof furnace
    
- Required for:
    
    - Superior potions
        
    - All decoctions
        
    - Mutagen infusion rituals
        

Cost: **300₡** (rare)  
Usage fee: **20₡/hr**

---

## **🔮 ARCANE LAB (Mage Tower, Witcher Keep)**

- Arcane circles
    
- Energy stabilizers
    
- Infusion channels
    
- Required for:
    
    - Relic-grade alchemy
        
    - Mutagen mutations
        
    - Magic item repair
        
    - Leyline-powered brews
        

Cost: priceless (quest-only)  
Usage: GM-gated resource

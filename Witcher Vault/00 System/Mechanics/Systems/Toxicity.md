# **POTION TOXICITY & OVERDOSE MECHANIC**

A faithful adaptation of Witcher toxicity.

---

## **TOXICITY SCORE (TS)**

Each potion has a Toxicity value:

- **Common:** 1 TS
    
- **Enhanced:** 2 TS
    
- **Superior:** 3 TS
    
- **Decoction:** 4 TS
    
- **Relic:** 5 TS
    

### **MAX TOXICITY = CON mod + Proficiency + 4**

---

## **EFFECTS OF OVERDOSE**

When TS exceeds max:

|Overdose Level|Effect|
|---|---|
|+1–2 TS|Disadvantage on ability checks|
|+3–4 TS|Disadvantage on attacks & saves|
|+5 TS|1 lvl exhaustion & hallucinations|
|+6+ TS|Collapse → unconscious 1d4 hrs|

---

## **PURGING TOXICITY**

- Rest: reduce by Con mod per hour
    
- “White Honey”: instantly drops TS to 0
    
- Vomiting (forced purge): drop TS by 2, take 1d6 damage

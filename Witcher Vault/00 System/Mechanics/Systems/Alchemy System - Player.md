## **🎒 WHAT YOU NEED TO CRAFT**

- A **base alcohol** (Dwarven Spirit, Albedo, Rubedo, Nigredo)
    
- **Monster components** (blood, glands, mutagens)
    
- **Herbs** (assumed gathered or bought)
    
- **Alchemy tools** or a **crafting station**
    

---

|Tier|DC|Time|Notes|

|Common|12|10 min|Basic Witcher potions|
|Enhanced|14|30 min|Improved potency|

|Decoction|18|2 hr|Mutagen-powered|
|Relic|20+|4 hr|Legendary|

---

## **⚗️ BASIC INGREDIENT TYPES**

- **Common Component (CC):** basic monster bits
    
- **Alchemical Extract (AE):** special organs, essences
    
- **Mutagen (M):** powerful core reagent
    

---

## **🧬 ALCHEMY CATEGORIES & REQUIREMENTS**

### **Potions**

- 1 CC + herbs
    
- Alcohol base
    
- Effect boosts (healing, perception, stamina)
    

### **Oils**

- 1 AE + 1 CC
    
- Applied to weapons
    
- Bonus damage vs monster types
    

### **Bombs**

- 1 AE + 1 explosive base
    
- Area effects, elemental bursts
    

### **Decoctions**

- 1 Mutagen + rare herbs + alcohol
    
- Long-lasting strong effects
    
- Carry risk (mutagen toxicity)
    

---

## **🔧 FAILING A CRAFT CHECK**

- Fail by 1–4: weaker result
    
- Fail by 5+: lose half materials
    
- Critical fail: explosion, poison, etc.

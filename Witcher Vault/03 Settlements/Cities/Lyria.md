# Description
Lyria is the proud and fortified capital of the United Kingdom of Lyria & Rivia.  
Built atop rolling hills and overlooking fertile river valleys, the city stands as a symbol of Queen Meve’s rule — orderly, disciplined, and fiercely loyal.  
Lyria’s stone architecture, gleaming citadel, and bustling markets create an atmosphere of structured prosperity tinged with the lingering anxieties of recent wars against Nilfgaard.  
The city is clean, organized, and heavily patrolled, though political tension simmers beneath its polished surface.

---

# Geography
Lyria sits in central Lyria, surrounded by:
- fertile farmlands  
- rolling green hills  
- shallow foothills rich in silver  
- two major roads connecting Rivia, Aedirn, and Mahakam  

The climate is cool, with fog-heavy mornings and mild summers.  
The nearby **Whisper Mines** supply much of the kingdom’s silver but are notorious for collapses and monster sightings.

# Government
Lyria is ruled by **Queen Meve**, though she is often away leading military efforts.  
In her absence, authority is delegated to:

- **Royal Steward Aldren Var Tress**, who administers law and taxes  
- **The Small Council**, composed of nobles, merchants, generals, and clergy  
- **Marshal Gellert Vorlic**, commander of the Lyrian Armed Forces  

Justice is harsh, efficient, and rooted in long-standing Lyrian law.  
Corruption is low but not nonexistent — particularly along the docks.

---

# Defenses
Lyria is among the best-defended cities in the North.

- Triple-tier stone walls surrounding the central city  
- Veteran garrison, including cavalry and infantry  
- Two fortified gates and watchtowers across the ridge line  
- Elite cavalry stables producing some of the best horses in the North  
- Strict licensing of mages  
- A contingency plan for Nilfgaardian invasion  

Monsters rarely breach the inner farmlands but are a threat in outer villages.

# Economy
Lyria’s economy is strong and diverse:

**Major industries:**  
- Silver mining  
- Horse breeding  
- Weapon and armor forging  
- Textile production  
- Grain and wool from surrounding villages  

**Trade partners:**  
- Rivia  
- Aedirn  
- Temeria  
- Mahakam  

**Current economic issues:**  
- Mine collapses disrupting silver production  
- Banditry in outer regions  
- War reparations straining city coffers  

---

# History

**Founding:**  
Lyria was founded nearly a millennium ago as a fortified hill settlement.  
It grew into a capital as the Kingdom expanded under the Meve dynasty.

**Major Events:**  
- Early border wars with Aedirn  
- Union with Rivia under House Meve  
- Repeated Nilfgaard invasions, several resisted  
- The “Lyrian Cavalry Charge,” a legendary turning of battle  
- Recent occupation and liberation during the Third Nilfgaard War  

Today, Lyria is still recovering from economic and psychological scars.

---

# Culture
Lyrians value:
- tradition  
- loyalty  
- military service  
- discipline  
- cautious pragmatism  

They view magic suspiciously, though not with Temerian zealotry.  
Music, cavalry parades, and harvest festivals are common.

**Major festivals:**  
- Day of the First Charge  
- Spring Ribbon Festival  
- Vigil of Melitele  

---

# Religion
**Melitele** remains the dominant faith, with temples and shrines throughout the city.  
Smaller cults worship:
- river spirits  
- ancestor guardians  
- local protective deities  

The **Eternal Fire** maintains a small but ambitious chapel, attempting to recruit.

### **The Claw of the Forgotten Bear**

**Region:** Northern **Spikeroog**, village **Weddle**  
**Travel Distance:** ~300 miles (4–5 weeks travel by horseback, 6–8 on foot)

**Hook:**  
The adventurers arrive at the decayed fortress known locally as the **Old Hall**, hidden deep within pine forests north of the Pontar. Once a Bear School keep, it fell long before the Conjunction. The place reeks of blood and cold steel.

**Complication:**  
A **mutated bear wraith**, once a Bear School master, guards the fortress. His death curse fused his spirit and flesh into something monstrous. Within lies the relic — a steel talisman that grants incredible strength at the cost of one’s sanity.

**Moral Twist:**  
A surviving (or ghostly) Bear School witcher warns the group that the _Claw_ is both a weapon and a prison. Taking it binds them to the spirit’s rage. Will they destroy the relic, use it, or return it to Kaer Morhen’s vaults?

**Travel Hook:**  
Before leaving, the witcher’s spirit speaks of a **“Crooked Tree”**, a corrupted druidic grove far to the north in the **Caed Dhu Forest**, where children have vanished. The curse there, he says, “echoes the same darkness that damned me.”

**Hook**: Peasants beg for help — their crops rot overnight, and livestock are found bloodless.
**Complication**: It’s not a vampire — it’s a leshen, angered by deforestation and hunting of its forest kin.
**Moral Twist**: The peasant elder secretly ordered the cutting of sacred trees to sell the wood for weapons. Do the players kill the leshen or the greedy elder?
**Location:** Barefield near Velen

**Region:** Outskirts of **Barefield Near**, northeast of Velen  
**Travel Distance:** ~250 miles (3–4 weeks travel by horseback, 5–6 on foot)

**Hook:**  
Farmers in the Velhad countryside plead for help — crops rot in a single night, livestock are found drained of blood, and an eerie silence grips the woods. They speak of a vampire.

**Complication:**  
The true cause is a **leshen** angered by deforestation — hunters and loggers cut down ancient groves under orders from the village elder, who secretly sells the wood to Nilfgaardian blacksmiths for weapon stocks.

**Moral Twist:**  
The elder’s greed caused the curse. Will the party slay the leshen or expose the elder, risking the wrath of starving villagers who depend on him?

**Travel Hook:**  
As the group leaves Velhad, they encounter a **Nilfgaardian courier** searching for a missing arms shipment. Among the documents is a mention of a _Bear School relic_ — the **Claw of the Forgotten Bear**, reportedly found near the ruins of an old fortress by the Pontar River.

**Sylaen, Forest Spirit (Unique)**  
Medium Fey (Spirit) • **Armor Class** 15 (bark-hide)  
**Hit Points** 68 (minimum threshold for a deadly fight)  
**Speed** 20 ft., burrow 10 ft. (roots)

**STR** 18 (+4)  
**DEX** 12 (+1)  
**CON** 16 (+3)  
**INT** 10 (+0)  
**WIS** 15 (+2)  
**CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saving Throws** Wis +4, Con +5  
**Skills** Perception +4, Nature +3  
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from non-silvered weapons  
**Damage Vulnerabilities** fire  
**Damage Immunities** poison  
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned  
**Senses** tremorsense 30 ft., darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14  
**Languages** Druidic, Sylvan; understands Common but speaks rarely  
**Proficiency Bonus** +2

---

## **TRAITS**

### **Bound to the Grove**

Sylaen cannot leave the Crooked Tree’s grove. If forced more than 40 ft. from the tree, it becomes **stunned** until returned or until the tree is destroyed.

### **Root-Warden**

While Sylaen stands on natural ground, it can use a **bonus action** to cause roots to erupt, granting it half cover (+2 AC) until the start of its next turn.

### **Spirit of Grief**

The area within 30 ft. of the Crooked Tree is a zone of oppressive sorrow.  
Creatures entering for the first time or starting their turn there must succeed on a **DC 12 Wisdom save** or suffer **disadvantage on their next attack**.

### **Unstable Essence**

When Sylaen is reduced to **0 HP**, it does not die unless the Crooked Tree is also destroyed. Otherwise, it reforms with 1 HP in 1 minute.

---

## **ACTIONS**

### **Multiattack**

Sylaen makes **two attacks**: one with **Grasping Limb** and one with **Root Lash**, or two Root Lashes.

---

### **Grasping Limb** (Melee Weapon Attack)

+6 to hit • reach 10 ft • one creature  
**Hit:** 9 (1d10+4) bludgeoning damage  
Target must succeed on a **DC 13 Strength save or be grappled** (escape DC 13).  
While grappled, the target is restrained and takes **4 (1d6)** bludgeoning damage at the start of each of its turns.

---

### **Root Lash** (Melee Weapon Attack)

+6 to hit • reach 15 ft • one creature  
**Hit:** 8 (1d8+4) slashing damage  
If the attack hits, the roots pull the creature **5 ft. toward the Crooked Tree**.

---

### **Summon Thorn Wisps (Recharge 5–6)**

Sylaen summons **1d2 Thorn Wisps** (tiny flying briar-spirits).  
Each has 5 HP, AC 13, +4 to hit, 3 piercing damage, and vanish at 0 HP.  
Their purpose is harassment: disrupting casters, flanking, and generally stressing the party.

---

### **Weeping Pulse (1/Day)**

Sylaen releases a wave of crushing spiritual grief.  
All creatures within 20 ft. must make a **DC 13 Wisdom save** or take **10 (3d6) psychic damage** and fall **prone**.  
Success halves damage and negates prone.

---

## **REACTIONS**

### **Root Snare**

When a creature Sylaen can see moves more than 10 ft. in a turn, Sylaen may lash roots from the ground.  
Creature must succeed a **DC 12 Dex save** or be **restrained until the start of its next turn**.

---

## **LEGENDARY ACTIONS (Optional for Extra Deadliness)**

Sylaen gets **1 legendary action** per round:

**Root Flick (Costs 1 Action)**  
A restrained creature takes **4 (1d6+1) bludgeoning damage** and is pulled 5 ft closer to the tree.

(If you want it harder: give it **2** legendary actions.)

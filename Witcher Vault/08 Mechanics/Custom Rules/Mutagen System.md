# **3. MUTAGEN MUTATION SYSTEM (Player Buff Progression)**

Inspired directly by Witcher 3 but redesigned for TTRPG balance.

You gain **Mutation Slots** (MS).  
Normal characters: **0–2 MS**  
Witchers: **start with 3 MS, can gain up to 6**

Each mutation requires a **Mutagen + Decoction** to unlock permanently.

---

## **🟥 RED MUTATIONS (Combat / Strength)**

|Mutation|Cost|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Muscle Memory**|1 red mutagen|+1 weapon damage|
|**Metabolic Control**|1 rare mutagen|+10% max stamina|
|**Killer Instinct**|1 epic mutagen|+1d4 damage vs wounded foes|
|**Euphoria (Greater)**|1 relic mutagen|+1 attack & +1 damage when under any potion|

---

## **🟦 BLUE MUTATIONS (Magic / Signs)**

|Mutation|Cost|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Mind Over Matter**|1 blue mutagen|+1 spell DC or sign intensity|
|**Magic Harmony**|1 rare|Potions reduce spell failure chance|
|**Cryostasis**|1 epic|On crit, freeze target (1/short rest)|
|**Conductivity (Greater)**|1 relic|When hit by element, gain resistance for 1 minute|

---

## **🟩 GREEN MUTATIONS (Alchemy / Vitality)**

|Mutation|Cost|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Tissue Regeneration**|1 green mutagen|+1 HP regen / round in combat|
|**Endure Pain**|1 rare|+5 max HP|
|**Synergy**|1 epic|Oils applied last twice as long|
|**Metamorphosis (Greater)**|1 relic|When taking potion damage, gain +2 to any stat for 1 min|

---

## **⚠️ MUTAGEN TOXICITY**

Each active mutation adds **1 Toxicity**  
Max Toxicity = **Con mod + proficiency**  
Exceeding it = exhaustion, hallucinations, or wraith-visions (GM choice)

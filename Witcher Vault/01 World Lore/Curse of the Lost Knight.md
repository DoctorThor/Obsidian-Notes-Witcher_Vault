# `=this.file.name`

The curse of the lost knight is the curse placed upon the sword and armor of a knight who couldn't make it back to his liege lord. 
## Acquisition
Any person who takes the arms or armor from the knights grave shall be afflicted with the curse.

## Effects

### Sword
If someone steals the sword from the lost knights grave. They will never willingly relinquish it to anyone but the liege lord. They will also have their life force sapped away by the curse at the rate of 1d4 per week.

### Armor
If someone should steal the armor of the lost knight they will loose some of their vitality for as long as the armor is not returned. They will also become and astral beacon drawing in any spectral undead within 360 ft to attack them.

## Cure
In order for the curse to be removed from someone who has gotten it from taking the arms and armor of the knight is to return what they took to King Henselt of Kaedwen.

## Character Summary

Indra Taran is a Thunder Dwarf from Mahakam — violet-skinned, silver-eyed, with silvery-blue hair and stone fists and forearms from consuming earth elemental essence. A failed merchant turned adventurer, she carries the pragmatic cunning of someone who has negotiated across a table and lost, and the raw physical presence of someone who settled disputes the other way afterward. She is traveling with a mixed non-human party in human-dominated territory, which is either the most natural thing in the world to her or a daily reminder of exactly how far from Mahakam she is.

---

## Appearance

**Height / Build:** 3'6", 220 lbs. Stocky even by dwarf standards. Dense.
**Distinguishing Features:** Violet skin. Silver eyes. Silvery-blue hair. Stone fists and forearms — the flesh has hardened to something that looks and feels like granite from the elbow down. Impossible to miss or mistake.
**How they present in hostile territory:** Her stone arms are not concealable. She walks into every settlement already marked. Whether she leans into that or tries to deflect it with merchant's charm is a session-by-session question — her Failed Merchant background gives her the tools for both.

---

## Personality & Roleplay Notes

**Core traits:** Chaotic Neutral. Galavan faith. Practical, direct, not easily embarrassed.
**Under pressure:** Rages. Literally. The Storm Aura activates and lightning starts finding targets.
**Toward non-humans:** Solidarity by default — she knows what it costs to travel as something humans stare at.
**Toward humans:** Transactional. She'll work with them, sell to them, drink with them. Doesn't trust them until they've earned it.
**Toward authority:** Skeptical. A failed merchant has dealt with enough toll collectors and guild inspectors to have opinions about authority.
**Toward the witcher:** TBD — develop through play.
**Secrets or things they haven't told the party:** Background details to be developed.

## Non-Human Situation

**Race and how it reads in human territory:** Impossible to conceal. Violet skin, silver eyes, and stone arms from the elbow down. She is visually extraordinary even among non-humans. Humans who have never seen a Thunder Dwarf don't know what they're looking at, which can be more dangerous than recognition.
**How they handle the gate tax / refused service:** Merchant background means she knows how to negotiate, argue, and charm. Whether she uses those tools or lets her fists do the talking depends on the situation and her mood.
**History with human institutions:** Unknown — to be developed.
**Relationship with their own people:** Left Mahakam. The nature of that departure — voluntary, forced, or something in between — is part of her backstory.

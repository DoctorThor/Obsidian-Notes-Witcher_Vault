**Help Needed — A Curse Upon Our Village!**

No children born here these past ten years.  
Wombs dry, babes stillborn — the gods have turned their faces.  
The wise woman says a spirit in the forest laid this curse after the hunters killed a dryad near the Crooked Tree.

We beg any who know the old ways to aid us. Find the spirit, lift the curse, or at least bring us peace from its wrath.

Speak with **Matka Renna**, our midwife, in Duskfields for the full tale.

_Reward: 250 crowns and the gratitude of every soul still breathing in this village._  
_(A child’s ribbon is tied to the bottom of the notice.)_
